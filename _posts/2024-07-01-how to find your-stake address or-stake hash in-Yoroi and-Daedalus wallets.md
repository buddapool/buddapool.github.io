Looking at the top list and don't know who is you? Follow this guidance may help you to find your Stake Address.
<h4>I. How to find address in Yoroi wallet</h4>
<p><strong>Step 1. Go to {Receive}</strong><br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address1.png" width="400">
</p>
<p><strong>Step 2. Swipe to the next page</strong><br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address2.png" width="400">
</p>
<p><strong>Step 3. Your Stake Hash is here (Stake key hash)</strong>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address3.png" width="400">
</p>
<p><strong>Step 4. Copy your {Stake key hash}</strong><br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address4.png" width="400">
</p>
<p><strong>Step 5. Use your Web Browser, go to adastat.net and Select {Search}</strong><br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address5.png" width="400">
</p>
<p><strong>Step 6.  Paste your copied {Stake key hash} into Search box then Select your Staking account</strong><br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address6.png" width="400">
</p>
<p><strong>Step 7 (Final). Your {Stake Address} is here (Reward address)</strong><br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/buddapool/buddapool.github.io/refs/heads/main/assets/images/find-address7.png" width="400">
</p>
